############## PART 1 ############## 

In order to run the part 1 of the project we do:
1) go to google maps in order to choose your scanning location.
2) While in google maps, right click on the exact scanning location.
3) On the pop-up window click the coordinates label.
4) Now the coordinates are copied, go to terminal.
5) Execute the following(using the arguments you just copied):
	e.g. /root/wrapper_part1.sh 30.3 35.4

!!!!! Note !!!!!
In order to execute correctly the program you must remove the comma 
while paste the coordinates as arguments in the above script.
	e.g. /root/wrapper_part1.sh 30.3, 35.4 ---> e.g. /root/wrapper_part1.sh 30.3 35.4
